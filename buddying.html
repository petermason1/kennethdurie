Buddying through difficult times

Our buddying program pairs individuals with trained support buddies to provide companionship, emotional support, and practical assistance during challenging periods.

One-on-One Support

When facing difficult times, having someone to talk to and lean on can make all the difference. Our buddying program matches individuals with compassionate, trained buddies who offer understanding, encouragement, and practical support. Whether you're navigating a new diagnosis, dealing with daily challenges, or simply need someone to listen, our buddies are here to help you through.
