Meet Up and Chat at Ken's Coffee Mornings

Join us for friendly coffee mornings at Backworth Club. A welcoming space to meet, chat, and connect with others in our community.

About Our Coffee Mornings

Ken's Coffee Mornings provide a relaxed and friendly environment where people can come together, share experiences, and build connections. Whether you're looking for support, friendship, or just a chat over a cup of coffee, everyone is welcome.

Location & Details

Venue: Backworth Club

What to expect: Friendly conversation, hot drinks, and a welcoming atmosphere where you can meet new people and catch up with friends.

Free food and cuppas available! Come along and enjoy complimentary refreshments while you chat and connect with others.

Our coffee mornings are a great opportunity to:

  • Meet others in the community
  • Share experiences and support each other
  • Enjoy a relaxed social environment
  • Learn about local resources and services
  • Build lasting friendships

Share Your Hidden Talents

Our coffee mornings are also a place to share your hidden talents! Whether you have a skill, hobby, or creative ability you'd like to showcase or discuss, we'd love to hear about it. This is a supportive space where everyone's talents are celebrated.

From music and art to crafts, cooking tips, or any other special skills - feel free to share what makes you unique. You never know who might be inspired or who might share a similar interest!

Getting Involved

For more information about upcoming coffee mornings, times, and dates, please check our social media channels or contact us through the contact page. We'd love to see you there!
