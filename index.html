Ken Durie Mentoring and Advice Systems

Ken Durie is now looking to represent any disabled people who are struggling. Through the Ken Durie mentoring and advice systems, we use AI to analyze trends and identify places where disabled people are being forgotten about by greedy corporations who are more interested in making money than caring for the people of this country.

We fight for those who have been overlooked, providing support, advocacy, and a voice for disabled individuals who deserve better. Our AI-powered analysis helps us uncover systemic issues and hold corporations accountable for their failures to support disabled people.
